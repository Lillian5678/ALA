The Doodling Robot









A tiny doodling companion

This fun toy that you can assemble yourself for ages 8+ is a way to train a young child's hand-eye coordination, and also allows kids ages 12+ to practice the ability of adjusting the angle inclination of the pen to that will help them create a piece of art with more than just circles and other shapes. It enhances creativity, and imagination. By altering the length of the arms, the orientation, and the angle and make a work of contemporary art. All you need is a AA battery, set up the bot, and take it for a spin!
